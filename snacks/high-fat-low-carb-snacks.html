High-Fat Snacks That Keep You Full

If you find yourself constantly hungry, you aren't eating enough fat. Fat triggers satiety signals in the brain.

1. The "Fat Bomb"

These are bite-sized treats made of coconut oil, butter, nut butter, and sweetener. Kept in the freezer, they melt in your mouth and provide instant energy.

2. Avocado

Eat half an avocado with a spoon. Sprinkle with salt and lime juice. Loaded with fiber and monounsaturated fat.

3. Heavy Cream Coffee

Not technically food, but a coffee with 2 tablespoons of heavy cream has enough calories to act as a snack.

4. Macadamia Nuts

The king of nuts. They have the highest fat-to-carb ratio of any nut.
