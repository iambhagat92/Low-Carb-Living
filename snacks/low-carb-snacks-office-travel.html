Low-Carb Snacks for Office & Travel

The office vending machine is a danger zone. Travel plazas are full of chips. You need snacks that are shelf-stable and don't require a fridge.

1. Canned Sardines/Tuna

Keep a few tins in your desk drawer. In an emergency, it's a perfect protein meal.

2. Dark Chocolate

A bar of 85% or 90% dark chocolate keeps well in a bag (as long as it's not hot) and satisfies cravings.

3. Beef Sticks

Individually wrapped beef sticks (like Chomps or Archer's) are lifesavers for road trips.

4. Nut Butter Packets

Single-serve squeeze packs of almond butter. No spoon required.
