10 Easy Low-Carb Recipes for Weight Loss

Think low-carb means boring chicken and broccoli? Think again. One of the reasons low-carb diets are so successful is that the food is genuinely satisfying. You get to eat cheese, butter, and bacon!

Here are 10 simple, family-friendly recipes you can whip up in under 30 minutes.

Dinner Winners

1. Zucchini Lasagna

The Swap: Roasted zucchini slices replace noodles.

Layer grilled zucchini strips with ricotta cheese, ground beef marinara sauce (no added sugar!), and top with mozzarella. Bake until bubbly.

2. Cauliflower Fried Rice

The Swap: Grated cauliflower replaces white rice.

Sauté riced cauliflower in butter with garlic and ginger. Add diced ham, peas, and scrambled eggs. Soy sauce brings it all together.

3. Bunless Burger Bowls

The Swap: A big salad bowl replaces the bun.

A juicy beef patty topped with melted cheddar, pickles, tomatoes, and "special sauce" (mayo + sugar-free ketchup) over a bed of crispy lettuce.

4. Taco Skillet

Brown ground beef with taco spices. immense flavour. Serve with salsa, sour cream, and guacamole. Scoop it up with cucumber slices or cheese crisps.

5. Pizza Chicken

Use a butterfly chicken breast as the "crust." Top with pizza sauce, pepperoni, and cheese. Bake until chicken is cooked through.

Simple Lunches

  • 6. Tuna Melt: Tuna salad on top of a tomato slice, melted cheese on top.
  • 7. Egg Roll in a Bowl: Cabbage and pork sausage stir-fry with sesame oil.
  • 8. Cobb Salad: Hard-boiled eggs, bacon, blue cheese, avocado, chicken.
  • 9. Turkey Roll-Ups: Deli turkey wrapped around cream cheese and pickle spears.
  • 10. Salmon & Asparagus: One-pan roast with lemon butter.
