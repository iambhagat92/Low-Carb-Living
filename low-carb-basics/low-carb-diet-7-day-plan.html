Low-Carb Diet for Beginners (7-Day Start Plan)

Starting is the hardest part. You know what to eat, but putting it into a schedule can be overwhelming. To make it easy, we've put together a simple week of eating.

Rules: Eat until you are satisfied. Don't count calories. Drink water. If you get hungry between meals, eat a handful of nuts or a piece of cheese.

Monday Breakfast: Two eggs fried in butter with bacon.
Lunch: Chicken salad (leftover chicken, mayo, celery, on greens).
Dinner: Pan-seared steak with steamed broccoli and butter.
Tuesday Breakfast: Omelet with cheese and spinach.
Lunch: Leftover steak slices with a side salad.
Dinner: Baked salmon with asparagus (roasted in olive oil).
Wednesday Breakfast: High-fat yogurt (Greek) with a few raspberries.
Lunch: Canned tuna mixed with mayo, wrapped in lettuce leaves.
Dinner: Pork chops with mashed cauliflower (use plenty of butter/cream).
Thursday Breakfast: Coffee with heavy cream (skip food if not hungry).
Lunch: Turkey burger patty (no bun) with cheese and avocado.
Dinner: Beef stir-fry (use frozen stir-fry veggies) with soy sauce.
Friday Breakfast: Boiled eggs (prep these ahead of time).
Lunch: Shrimp salad with olive oil dressing.
Dinner: Taco Bowl - Ground beef, taco seasoning, cheese, sour cream, salsa (no rice/chips).
Saturday Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with diced ham.
Lunch: Cold cut roll-ups (deli meat wrapped around cheese sticks).
Dinner: Roast chicken with skin on (eat the legs/wings!) and green beans.
Sunday Breakfast: Almond Flour Pancakes.
Lunch: Leftover roast chicken.
Dinner: Bunless cheeseburgers with pickles and mustard.

Keep Going!

Simple, right? You can repeat this, or mix and match meals. For more specific meal inspirations, visit 10 Easy Low-Carb Recipes.
